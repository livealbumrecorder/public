# Privacy Policy
Effective date: May 9 2026

##Overview
This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All functionality operates entirely on your device.

##Data Collection
No personal information is collected.
No analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs are used.
No account or login is required.

##Audio Recordings & Files
Audio recordings and any exported files are stored locally on your device only.
The app does not upload, transmit, or provide remote access to your files.
You have full control over creating, exporting, and deleting content.

##Permissions
The app may request the following permissions strictly for core functionality:
Microphone — to record audio.
Photos / Media / Gallery — to save exported audio files to your device’s media library (write access only; no background scanning or indexing).
Files / Storage — to allow you to export and save recordings to locations you choose (read/write only for user-initiated actions).
These permissions are used only when you explicitly perform related actions (e.g., recording or exporting). The app does not access files or media without your interaction.

##Data Sharing
No data is shared with third parties.

##Security
All data remains on your device. The app does not use remote servers.

##Children’s Privacy
The app does not collect any information from users, including children.

##Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected with a new effective date.
